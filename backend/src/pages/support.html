Familygram — Support

Last updated 2026-05-21

Familygram is a private, invite-only photo feed shared by a single family. Each family runs its own instance — there's no shared service. If you have a problem using the app, this page is the place to start.

Common questions

I can't sign in — it says my email isn't on the family list.

Familygram is invite-only. Ask the family member who invited you to add your Google email to the allowlist from their Profile screen. Once they do, sign in again and you'll be let through.

I signed in with the wrong Google account.

Tap your avatar on the Profile screen, then "Sign out", and sign in again with the right account. If the wrong account already created posts, contact the admin of your family's instance and they can clean it up.

Face ID / Touch ID isn't unlocking the app.

Open the iOS Settings app → Familygram → make sure Face ID is allowed. If it still fails, sign out from the Profile screen and sign in again — that resets the biometric binding.

I want to delete my account and all my data.

Open the Profile screen and tap "Delete my account". This removes your posts, comments, likes, and profile from the family's instance within seconds. It does not delete your Google account, which Familygram doesn't control.

I want a specific photo or comment removed.

Open the post or comment in the app and use the delete action (you can only delete content you posted). To request removal of content someone else posted about you, contact the admin of your family's instance.

Is Familygram free? Are there in-app purchases?

The app itself is free, with no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or paid content. The family member who set up the instance pays only for the Apple Developer Program ($99/year) so the app can be distributed via TestFlight / the App Store; running the backend is on Cloudflare's free tier for normal family-scale use.
